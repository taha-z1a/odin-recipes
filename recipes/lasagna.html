World's Best Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and tender sheets of pasta. This hearty meal is perfect for family dinners or special occasions. The secret to a great lasagna lies in slow-cooked tomato sauce and a perfect balance of cheese and herbs. Whether you're using ground beef, sausage, or going vegetarian, the dish always turns out comforting and satisfying.

Traditionally, lasagna is made with ricotta or béchamel sauce, mozzarella cheese, and grated Parmesan layered between sheets of lasagna noodles. After baking in the oven until bubbly and golden brown on top, it’s best served hot with garlic bread or a fresh green salad. Leftovers taste even better the next day, making it a meal that keeps on giving.

Ingredients

  • Meat: This super meaty lasagna has sweet Italian sausage and lean ground beef.
  • Onion and garlic: An onion and two cloves of garlic are cooked with the meat to add tons of flavor.
  • Tomato products: You'll need a can of crushed tomatoes, two cans of tomato sauce, and two cans of tomato paste.
  • Sugar: Two tablespoons of white sugar add subtle sweetness and enhance the flavor of the sauce.
  • Cheeses: Parmesan, mozzarella, and ricotta cheese make this lasagna extra decadent.

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
