Slow Cooker Chicken Tacos

Description

Slow cooker chicken tacos are a delicious, hands-off meal perfect for busy days. Simply combine boneless, skinless chicken breasts with your favorite taco seasoning, salsa, and a bit of lime juice in a slow cooker. As it simmers for several hours, the chicken becomes incredibly tender and flavorful, soaking up all the spices and juices. Once cooked, it easily shreds with a fork, making it ideal for tacos, burrito bowls, or even quesadillas.

This recipe is highly customizable — you can add diced onions, jalapeños, corn, or black beans to the slow cooker for extra flavor and texture. Serve the shredded chicken in soft or hard taco shells and top with your favorites like shredded lettuce, cheese, avocado, sour cream, or cilantro. It's a satisfying, crowd-pleasing meal with minimal effort.

Ingredients

  • Broth: Start with a cup of chicken broth (store-bought or homemade).
  • Taco seasoning: Use store-bought taco seasoning or make your own at home.
  • Chicken: A pound of skinless, boneless chicken breasts should make eight servings.

Steps

  1. Place chicken breasts in the slow cooker.
  2. Sprinkle taco seasoning over the chicken.
  3. Pour salsa and lime juice on top.
  4. Add optional onions, corn, and black beans if using.
  5. Cover and cook on low for 6–7 hours or high for 3–4 hours.
  6. Shred the chicken with two forks.
  7. Stir to mix with juices.
  8. Serve in taco shells with your favorite toppings.
